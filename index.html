The Body Knows

If cancer grows, it follows a path—cells divide, signals are sent, the immune system is bypassed.
But if illness follows a route, so must healing.
The same body that makes room for disease holds the knowledge to undo it.
The body isn’t confused. It isn’t broken. It’s responding to what it’s been given.
Change the input. Change the instructions. The body will follow.
The body knows the way home.
